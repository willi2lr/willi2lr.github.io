About Me

Here's My Story

Hello again! I graduated from James Madison University in May 2015 with a degree in Computer Information Systems (CIS). Right out of college, I immediately landed an IT Risk Advisory Consulting job at one of the big four accounting firms, EY. Although, I started my career in this position, I know that my heart and brain were originally wired to create, build, develop, and solve problems. I am currently looking for a job that will allow me to gain experience in front-end and back end development. I want to create beautiful and useful websites and applications that make everyone's lives easier. In the meantime, I will continue to expand my programming skills with learning Ruby on Rails, HTML, and CSS using Codeacademy and Github. I am most comfortable with ASP.NET and C# but I am eager to challenge myself and grow my skill set with learning new languages. In my free time, I practice my coding, design websites and graphic flyers for friends and for my sorority, Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Inc., read, workout, and try new recipes. I have been involved with organizations such as Women Who Code DC, Women in Technology (WIT), Association of Information Technology Professionals (AITP), and Technology Student Association (TSA). I am hoping to attend my first Hackathon very soon!
