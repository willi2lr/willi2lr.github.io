C.U.R.L.S Club Website

Project Details

I created this website for a club I established at James Madison University. The club is called C.U.R.L.S and it is a curly hair club for anyone with curly hair. It stands for Confidence. Understanding. Respect. Learning. Support. The club needed a website to keep connected with members and for advertisement purposes. I used wix to create this website.
