KPMG Magellan

Project Details

For my capstone course project in college, I specifically worked on a small team to develop a travel booking system for KPMG. We used ASP.Net, C#, and SQL Server to design, plan, and develop a way for employees to search for and book a flight, car, hotel, train, or a bundle package. We also added the functionality to log in as an employee or intern, manage user profile, view reservation history, export reservation history to Excel, and email the itinerary and confirmation message. The most rewarding part of developing this system was being able to utilize my problem solving skills once the requirements were identified and also seeing the customer oriented solutions and results. I enjoyed being able to docuement the client's requirements. In this project, the client was KPMG. We interviewed the client to gather requirements and ask questions about design and functionality. After defining requirements, we began our planning phase. We documented a UML Diagram, ERD Diagram, Use Case Scenarios, Use Case Diagrams, and a storyboard. After continuously refining our plan and frequent follow ups with the client, we developed the user interface using ASP.NET. Next we added in the functionality and operation using C# and we connected the system to the database that we created from scratch. I had a hand in all aspects of the planing and development of the system. Myself and one other team member were in charge of the back-end system development an database desgin and managaement. The most challenging part of this project was managing scope creep. Overrall I truly enjoyed developing this project. It was fun, challenging, and allowed me to put my problem solving skills into major action. Our professor loved our system and our system was actually one of the few top ranked in the course section.
