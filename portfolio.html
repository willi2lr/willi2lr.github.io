College Project: KPMG Magellan

For my capstone course project in college, I specifically worked on a small team to develop a travel booking system for KPMG. We used ASP.Net, C#, and SQL Server to design, plan, and develop a way for employees to search for and book a flight, car, hotel, train, or a bundle package. We also added the functionality to log in as an employee or intern, manage user profile, view reservation history, export reservation history to Excel, and email the itinerary and confirmation message. The most rewarding part of developing this system was being able to utilize my problem solving skills once the requirements were identified and also seeing the customer oriented solutions and results.
